TOP PLAYS

There are many great and famous matches of chess. Kasparov v Topalov 1999, Bobby Fischer v Donald Byrne 1956, and any game GothamChess plays. This match was no different. I was playing my good friend, who had been thwacking me the entire match. I was down on stockfish by 9.7, almost certain defeat from a mathematical standpoint. However, I spotted a possible mate in 3, and through the greatest rook sacrifice of my career, I overcame impossible odds.
